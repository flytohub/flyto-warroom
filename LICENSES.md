Flyto2 Warroom CE is an aggregate source-available distribution generated from
the Flyto2 workspace. It is not OSI-approved open-source software.

- `packages/flyto-code`: PolyForm Noncommercial 1.0.0
- `packages/flyto-contracts`: PolyForm Noncommercial 1.0.0

## What is allowed?

The license permits noncommercial purposes. It expressly includes qualifying
personal research, experiment, testing, study, hobby, and other listed personal
uses without anticipated commercial application. It also permits use by the
listed charitable, educational, public research, public safety or health,
environmental protection, and government organizations.

## What requires a separate written commercial license?

Commercial use is not granted by the CE license. This includes, without
limitation:

- internal production use by or for a for-profit business;
- offering a hosted service, SaaS, managed security service, or paid support;
- using CE in paid consulting, client delivery, or professional services;
- resale, OEM/white-label distribution, embedding in a commercial product, or
  charging for access; and
- any other use intended for commercial advantage or monetary compensation.

For-profit evaluation is not automatically licensed by PolyForm
Noncommercial. Obtain written evaluation permission or a commercial license
from the Flyto2 maintainer before using CE on behalf of a business.

The complete license text in each Flyto2-owned package controls over this FAQ.
This summary is not legal advice.

## What about earlier releases?

`v0.1.0` and `v0.1.1` were published under Apache-2.0. Their already-granted
Apache-2.0 rights are not revoked. The PolyForm Noncommercial restriction
applies to the next release and later Flyto2-owned CE material unless a later
release says otherwise.
